ABOUT

My name is Traian Munteanu and I am a web developer. Everyday I solve problems with clarity, simplicity and honesty, combining digital craftsmanship with innovative thinking to deliver beautifully built digital experiences for clients worldwide.

I have a background in marketing and advertising but I decided to make the switch to web development about three years ago feeling more found of this craft. My area of expertise is small to medium companies presentational/catalogue websites, eCommerce applications and I am currently learning a lot about mobile development.
